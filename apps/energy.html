Documents to have ready
Caderneta predial, certidão de registo predial, ficha técnica da habitação (if available), floor plan.
Access needed
Perito must access every room, plus heating/cooling/water-heating equipment and its data plates.
Typical turnaround
2–5 business days after the site visit, depending on size and system complexity.
Validity
10 years for residential and small commercial buildings.
Legal requirement
Mandatory before advertising a sale or rental. Fines run €250–3,740 for individuals, up to €44,890 for companies.
2026 update: from May 2026, EU renovation-passport obligations begin applying to properties rated E or lower. Worth flagging early to sellers of older, lower-rated stock.

Need a real energy certificate?

We coordinate with ADENE-certified peritos qualificados for the official SCE certificate. Required for sale or rental — typically €120–€700 + IVA depending on property size.
