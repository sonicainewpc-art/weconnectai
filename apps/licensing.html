Legal context is moving right now. RJUE deadlines changed under Decreto-Lei 10/2024, changed again under Decreto-Lei 108/2026, and a further amendment bill is in progress. Default day-counts below are a general baseline — always edit them to match what's actually in force, and confirm with the câmara or a lawyer. This tool tracks elapsed time against whatever deadline you set; it does not certify what the current law says.
