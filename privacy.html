Privacy policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

1. Who we are

WeconnectAi is a civil engineering and pre-screening service based in the Algarve, Portugal. The data controller is [your name / your company name], with NIF [your NIF], reachable at geral@weconnectai.pt.

2. What data we collect

  • Contact form: name, email, phone (if provided), and the free-text description of your project.
  • App inputs: the data you type into the free apps (municipality, plot area, etc.). These are processed live by the Anthropic API as the AI provider. They are not stored on our servers.
  • App history (local storage): the apps save a copy of your last few screenings in your browser's localStorage so you can come back to them. This is in your browser only — not on our servers. You can clear it via your browser settings.
  • Cookies: essential cookies (language preference, session) are always on. Measurement cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) are only loaded after you click "Accept" in the cookie banner.

3. Why we use it (legal basis under GDPR)

  • Pre-contractual steps (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — to reply to your quote request and prepare a service proposal.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — to operate the website, prevent abuse, and improve the apps.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — for non-essential cookies and for any optional newsletter.

4. Anthropic API (the AI behind the apps)

The free apps use Anthropic's Claude API via a Cloudflare Worker proxy. When you click "Run screening", your inputs are sent to the Anthropic API. Anthropic's API does not use API requests to train models. See Anthropic's commercial terms and trust page for details.

5. Retention

Contact-form submissions: kept for 24 months unless a service contract is signed. App history in your browser: kept until you clear it.

6. Your rights (RGPD / GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to the processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with the CNPD (www.cnpd.pt). To exercise any of these, email geral@weconnectai.pt.

7. Security

The site is served over HTTPS. The contact form uses a mailto: link — your message is sent via your own email client (so we never see your SMTP credentials). The AI app calls are made over TLS through the Cloudflare Worker.

8. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

We use essential cookies (language, session) and — with your consent — measurement cookies. You can change your choice anytime in our privacy policy.
